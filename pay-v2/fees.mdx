title: "Fees"
description: "Learn more about Chargily Pay's fees."
---


Take advantage of a **0% fee** on all transactions during your first month. Then, you'll need to select the plan that aligns with your preferences, **including a FREE one.**

For additional information regarding our pricing and available plans, please visit [our pricing page](https://chargily.com/business/pay/pricing "our pricing page").

<Tip>The integration of Chargily Pay and testing it on your website or application is completely FREE.</Tip>

<Tip>For each transaction, you can choose to pay the fees yourself or pass them on to your customer. If you choose to pass them on, the fees will be added to the customer's total.</Tip>
